# LinkedIn

## Overview
<a href="https://www.linkedin.com/" target="_blank">LinkedIn</a> is a very popular website which acts as both a job board and a social networking site for professionals. For PSU CS students, it is very important to make your public brand look good. Try using LinkedIn to connect to some alumni, as they are more likely to accept a message request. Spend some time updating your LinkedIn to make it look professional. This will be the first professional impression many people have of you, so make it count!

## Information to Include
Try to add as much information about yourself as possible. Your resume is constrained in size, but your LinkedIn is not. If you had prior jobs or experiences that you can’t fit on your resume, you might as well include them here. Also try adding a short summary about yourself. This can tell future employers or colleagues what skills you have and what kind of person you are, so spend some time on this.

## Networking
If you’re applying for jobs, it's a good idea to put yourself out there. There’s no harm in trying to connect with someone. If someone’s working for a company you’re applying for, a recommendation could be just a few messages away. LinkedIn, as a social networking platform, also provides great opportunities to put yourself out there by creating or responding to posts. This could get you seen by a future employer and help lead to a job.

## Applying for Jobs
Many jobs / internships on LinkedIn are very easy to apply to, only requiring filling in a few boxes. Apply to these, but not only to these since these jobs will naturally have the most competition. LinkedIn provides rough estimates for how many people apply to jobs, so searching through the list to find jobs with fewer applicants may be a good idea.

## Tips
- You can use LinkedIn’s AI search feature to describe the type of job you want and find where you should apply to.
- Apply to as many positions as possible! Applying to safety positions is never a bad idea.
- See <a href="job-application-resources.html">Job Application Resources</a> for more info.
- For PSU specific job postings, check out <a href="https://app.joinhandshake.com/" target="_blank">Handshake</a>.
