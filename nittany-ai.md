# Nittany AI Alliance

## Overview
<a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/" target="_blank">Nittany AI</a> is a student-led club, offering four programs that create a clear pipeline from coursework to a paid internship. This includes their Leadership Academy, AI Challenge, Machine Learning Bootcamp, and Advance internship. For PSU students aiming for a job in AI, these experiences are invaluable. Firstly, their <a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/student-society/leadership-academy" target="_blank">Leadership Academy</a> helps build leadership / project management skills. Their <a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/programs/challenge" target="_blank">AI Challenge</a> allows you to find a team of students to complete an AI related project to potentially earn $5000+ in prize money.

## Machine Learning Bootcamp
I participated actively in their <a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/student-society/machine-learning-bootcamp" target="_blank">Machine Learning Bootcamp</a>. From my experience, we had 12 weekly meetings to teach us fundamental AI topics. Over this time, we were assigned 4 hands-on AI projects. This opportunity is great both for learning AI topics and to help flesh out your resume.

## Nittany AI Advance
You may then be able to use those skills from the bootcamp to secure a paid internship with their <a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/programs/advance" target="_blank">Advance program</a>. The pay is comparable to other campus jobs, but the experience gained is much greater. You will be placed on a team of 3-5 and assigned a client with whom you will have biweekly meetings. They will provide an assignment, which you will have to complete to the best of your abilities. Personally, I worked with Lockheed Martin, where we essentially created a research project. This project gave me real world AI experience and allowed me to significantly hone my skills.

## How to Get Involved
- Apply to the Leadership Academy / Bootcamp (applications typically open around August)
- Apply to Advance internship (applications open up early each semester)
- Monitor their <a href="https://nittanyai.psu.edu/events" target="_blank">events page</a>
